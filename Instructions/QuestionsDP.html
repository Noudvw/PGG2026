Instructions

After all group members have made their contributions to the group project, you can assign Deduction Points to each of the group members. For each Deduction Point you assign, 3 Points will be deducted from the total of the recipient, and 1 Point will be deducted from your total. You can assign between 0 and 10 Deduction Points to each group member.

Deduction Points - Example

- All players have made their contribution to the group project.
- The screen displays the results of the game so far.
- You reach the "Deduction Points" stage.

- You assign the following Deduction Points to player A, B, and C: 2, 0, and 3.
- This reduces your income by 5 Points.
- Other participants assign a total of 4 Deduction Points to you.
- This reduces your income by (4x3=) 12 Points.
- In this example, the total income reduction due to Deduction Points is (5+12=) 17 Points.


Please be aware that when many Deduction Points are assigned to you, your income might become negative. In that case, your income will be set to 0 (zero). You cannot end up with negative income.
